Manali - The Land of Snow

Manali is one of the most beautiful hill stations in India. Covered with snow-capped mountains, pine forests, and rivers, it is a perfect destination for adventure lovers.

Things to do:

  • Paragliding in Solang Valley
  • Visit Hadimba Temple
  • River Rafting in Beas River
  • Snow Activities at Rohtang Pass

Goa - The Beach Paradise

Goa is famous for its beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. It is the best place to enjoy with friends, relax at the beach, and try delicious seafood.

Things to do:

  1. Relax at Baga Beach
  2. Explore Fort Aguada
  3. Enjoy Water Sports
  4. Try Goan Food

Jaipur - The Pink City

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is full of historical monuments, forts, and palaces. It is the best place for history lovers and those who enjoy traditional Rajasthani culture.

Famous Attractions:

  • Hawa Mahal
  • Amber Fort
  • City Palace
  • Jantar Mantar
